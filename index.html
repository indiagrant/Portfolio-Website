Hey, I'm India

A Full-Stack Software Developer

|About

Hi there! I'm India, an English Lit grad turned Full-Stack Software Developer. Let me give you a glimpse into my journey into tech.

Growing up I was always quite embarrassed of being the "tech nerd". Whilst my friends were out partying, I was happily
tucked away at home, burning the midnight oil in epic gaming marathons with my cousins. My 17th birthday was the stuff of teenage
dreams - instead of asking for the latest fashion accessory or a fancy gadget, I asked my family to hook me up with computer
components: a shiny new GPU, CPU...throw in a couple of computer fans maybe! You guessed it, it was all so I could build my very
own PC. Yep, that's my kind of birthday celebration!

Fast forward to university, where I embarked on a journey studying English Literature. My tech obsession was a well-kept
secret until my astute flatmates spotted the behemoth of a PC in my room. Suddenly, the cat was out of the bag - they realised I had
a lot more tech knowledge than I'd ever let on. They even tried to persuade me to switch gears and dive into a Computer Science degree.
While I did entertain the idea, I couldn't muster the courage to take the plunge and defer my course for a year. In hindsight, I sometimes
wonder what might have been if I had made that leap.

But here I am, fully embracing my love for all things tech, and determined to turn that passion into my career. It's never too late to follow
your true calling, and I'm ready to make the most of it. So, welcome to my world, where my tech journey is just getting started, and I'm
thrilled to share it with you. Let's embark on this tech-filled adventure together!
